## Voice Guidelines for This Product

### Tone
- Direct, not corporate
- Confident, not arrogant
- Specific, not vague
- Helpful, not preachy

### Key phrases to use
- "commodity language" / "commodity phrases"
- "sounds like everyone else"
- "buyers default to price"
- "copy-paste ready"
- "specific fixes" (not "recommendations")

### Key phrases to avoid
- "best practices"
- "optimize your proposal"
- "leverage your strengths"
- "value proposition" (ironic if we use it)
- Any phrase that appears in the commodity phrase database

### Perspective
- Always "you/your" not "the user/customers"
- Lee speaks in first person when natural ("I'll review..." not "Lee will review...")
- Empathy for the frustration ("We just lost a $300K bid")
- No corporate hedging ("may help" → "helps")
